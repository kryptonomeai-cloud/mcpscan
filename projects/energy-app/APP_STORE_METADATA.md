## Full Description
Take control of your electricity costs with Volt — the smart energy monitor built for UK households. Whether you're on a fixed tariff, Octopus Agile, or any other plan, Volt gives you real-time visibility into what you're spending and actionable ways to save.

**Smart Meter Integration**
Connect your smart meter in seconds using your Octopus Energy API key or n3rgy consumer access. Volt pulls your half-hourly consumption data automatically and presents it in beautiful, easy-to-read charts. See exactly how much electricity you've used today, this week, or this month — broken down by peak and off-peak hours.

**Tariff Comparison Engine**
Wondering if you're on the best deal? Volt's tariff comparison tool analyses your actual usage patterns and shows you how much you could save by switching. Compare fixed, variable, Agile, Tracker, and Cosy tariffs side by side with personalised savings estimates based on your real consumption data.

**EV Charging Optimisation**
If you drive an electric vehicle, Volt integrates with Ohme chargers to help you charge at the cheapest times. The smart charging scheduler finds the lowest-cost windows on your tariff and shows you exactly what each charge session costs. Track your charging history, monitor live charge status, and let Volt's AI find the best time to plug in.

**AI Energy Coach**
Volt's built-in AI coach analyses your usage patterns and delivers personalised tips to reduce your bills. Get daily insights on your energy habits, anomaly alerts when usage spikes unexpectedly, and smart recommendations tailored to your tariff and lifestyle.

**Carbon Footprint Tracking**
Monitor the carbon intensity of your electricity in real time. Volt connects to the National Grid Carbon Intensity API to show you when the grid is cleanest and calculates your personal Green Score. See how your energy choices impact the environment and track your progress over time with detailed carbon reports.

**Octopus Agile & Tracker Support**
For Octopus Energy customers on Agile or Tracker tariffs, Volt shows upcoming half-hourly rates so you can plan your usage around the cheapest periods. The rate timeline visualises price changes throughout the day, and the Best Time engine tells you exactly when to run your dishwasher, washing machine, or EV charger.

**Offline-First Design**
Volt caches your data locally so you can check your usage even without an internet connection. Your energy data syncs automatically when you're back online, and the app launches instantly with your most recent readings.

**Privacy-Focused**
Your energy data stays on your device. Volt doesn't sell your data or share it with third parties. API keys are stored securely using your device's encrypted keychain, and all network requests use HTTPS.

Volt is free to download and use. Upgrade to Volt Pro at £2.99/month to unlock advanced features including AI coaching, detailed carbon reports, multi-property support, and priority data sync.
