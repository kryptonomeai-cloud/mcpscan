# Content Strategy — TAYoutube

## Content Pillars (4 core themes)

### 1. 🤖 AI Builds (40% of content)
Real AI projects built on real hardware. Not "I tried ChatGPT" — actual deployments.

**Example videos:**
- "I Built an AI That Reads My Emails (Local LLM, No Cloud)"
- "Running 5 AI Models on 5 GPUs Simultaneously"
- "I Trained My Own AI Model in 5 Minutes (LoRA Fine-Tuning Explained)"
- "ComfyGPT: AI That Builds AI Workflows"
- "I Replaced Every Cloud Service With Local AI"
- "Building an AI Agent That Controls My Entire Homelab"
- "Fine-Tuning LLMs on Consumer GPUs — What Actually Works"

### 2. 🔒 Cybersecurity (25% of content)
Practical security — not fear-mongering. Defense AND offense.

**Example videos:**
- "I Scanned Every MCP Server for Vulnerabilities (MCPScan)"
- "My Homelab Blocked 14,000 Hackers Last Month (CrowdSec Setup)"
- "The Supply Chain Attack That Almost Got Me (Trivy Compromise)"
- "I Hacked My Own Network (and Fixed Everything I Found)"
- "Self-Hosting a Password Manager (Vaultwarden Setup)"
- "Every Developer Needs This Security Tool"

### 3. 🖥️ Homelab & Hardware (25% of content)
Building infrastructure. The stuff that makes everything else possible.

**Example videos:**
- "My 5x RTX 3090 AI Server Build (120GB VRAM!)"
- "I Migrated My Entire Setup to a New Machine (Live)"
- "Self-Hosting Everything: The Complete Guide"
- "Docker Containers I Can't Live Without"
- "Building a GPU Server for Under £2000"
- "NAS vs Cloud: I Tested Both for a Year"

### 4. ⚡ Quick Tech (10% — Shorts + quick hits)
60-second bangers. One concept, one punchline.

**Example Shorts:**
- "Run AI Locally in 60 Seconds"
- "This Linux Command Saved My Server"
- "The AI Tool No One Talks About"
- "3 Docker Containers Every Dev Needs"

## Video Formats

### Long-form (8-15 minutes) — 2x per week
The bread and butter. Tutorial/build format.

**Structure:**
1. **Hook** (0-15 sec): Show the end result or the problem
2. **Context** (15-60 sec): Why this matters, who it's for
3. **Build** (bulk): Step-by-step, show your screen + face cam
4. **Result** (30-60 sec): Demo the working thing
5. **What's next** (15 sec): Tease next video, subscribe CTA

### Shorts (30-60 seconds) — 3-5x per week
Quick tips, reactions, mind-blowing demos. These drive discovery.

**Structure:**
1. **Hook** (0-3 sec): Bold claim or visual
2. **Deliver** (3-45 sec): Show it
3. **Payoff** (last 5 sec): Result + "follow for more"

### Deep Dives (20-30 minutes) — 1x per month
Flagship content. Full project builds, comprehensive guides.

**Examples:**
- "I Built a Complete AI Lab From Scratch"
- "Self-Hosting in 2026: The Ultimate Guide"
- "From Zero to AI Developer: Everything You Need"

## Posting Schedule

### Phase 1: Launch (Weeks 1-4)
- **Goal:** 8-10 videos published, establish rhythm
- **Cadence:** 2 long-form + 3 Shorts per week
- **Focus:** Your strongest content pillar (AI Builds)

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 2-3)
- **Goal:** 30 videos, 1K subscribers
- **Cadence:** 2 long-form + 5 Shorts per week
- **Focus:** Diversify across all 4 pillars

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 4-6)
- **Goal:** 60+ videos, 10K subscribers
- **Cadence:** 3 long-form + 5 Shorts per week
- **Focus:** Collaborations, series, community

## Content Calendar System

Monthly planning cycle:
1. **Day 1:** Run trend scanner, review research folder
2. **Day 1-2:** Plan month's videos (titles, hooks, outlines)
3. **Weekly:** Batch film 2 long-form on one day
4. **Daily:** Film 1 Short (low effort, high volume)
5. **End of month:** Review analytics, adjust next month

Template: `content-calendar/YYYY-MM.md`

## Idea Backlog

Keep a running list in `research/video-ideas.md`. Every idea goes here. Score by:
- **Timeliness:** Is it trending NOW? (score 1-5)
- **Evergreen:** Will people search for this in 6 months? (1-5)
- **Uniqueness:** Can only YOU make this video? (1-5)
- **Effort:** How hard to film? (1=easy, 5=hard)

**Best videos:** High timeliness OR high evergreen + high uniqueness + low-medium effort.
