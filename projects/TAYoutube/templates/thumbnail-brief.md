**3 elements only:**
1. **Face:** Your expression (excited / shocked / focused / smirking)
2. **Text:** 3-5 words MAX, bold sans-serif, white or accent colour on dark
3. **Visual anchor:** The thing (hardware, terminal, logo, diagram)

## Size & Specs
- **Dimensions:** 1280 × 720 (16:9)
- **Max file size:** 2MB
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Safe zone:** Keep text/face away from edges (YouTube overlays timestamp + duration)

## Thumbnail Sins (Never Do These)
- ❌ More than 5 words
- ❌ Small text (must be readable at phone-screen size)
- ❌ Cluttered — if it's busy, it's invisible
- ❌ Same colours as YouTube's red UI (gets lost)
- ❌ No face (face thumbnails get 30%+ higher CTR)
- ❌ Fake expressions (people can tell)

## A/B Testing
After 48 hours, check CTR in YouTube Studio. If below 4%, try:
- Different text
- Different expression
- Simpler background
- Brighter accent colour
