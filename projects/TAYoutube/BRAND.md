# Brand Identity — T A

## The Persona

You're the mate who's deep into AI and cyber but explains things so anyone can follow. You build things on camera — not just talk about them. You get excited about tech, and that energy is contagious.

**You are NOT:**
- A lecturer reading slides
- A corporate "thought leader"
- Someone who says "smash that like button" every 30 seconds

**You ARE:**
- A builder who shows his work
- Someone who makes complex tech feel doable
- Genuinely enthusiastic (not performed enthusiasm)
- Occasionally funny, always authentic

---

## Tone & Voice

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| **Energy** | High but not manic. NetworkChuck energy, not Mr Beast screaming |
| **Language** | Conversational UK English. Say "right" and "let's crack on" not "awesome sauce" |
| **Technical depth** | Go deep but build up to it. Start simple, layer complexity |
| **Humour** | Dry wit, self-deprecating, occasional geeky jokes |
| **Pace** | Fast enough to keep attention, slow enough to follow |

### Catchphrases to develop (pick naturally):
- "Right, let's build this"
- "Here's where it gets interesting"
- "Now watch what happens"
- Something that becomes YOUR thing (will emerge naturally)

## Visual Identity

### Colours
- **Primary:** Dark theme (tech/hacker aesthetic)
- **Accent:** Electric blue or neon green (pick one, stay consistent)
- **Text:** White on dark, bold sans-serif

### Thumbnail Style (Critical — this is 80% of click-through)
Study these patterns from your inspirations:
- **NetworkChuck:** Face (surprised/excited) + bold text + tech visual. 3 elements max
- **Jakkuh:** Clean, minimal, face + one tech object + large text
- **Gabriel VIP:** Dark theme, neon accents, face close-up + text overlay

**Your formula:**
1. Your face showing genuine reaction (not fake shock)
2. 3-5 words MAX in bold text
3. One visual anchor (the thing you're building/showing)
4. Consistent colour palette across all thumbs
5. Dark background — makes text pop

### Channel Art
- Banner: Your name + tagline ("Building the future, one project at a time" or similar)
- Profile pic: Clean headshot or stylised avatar
- Watermark: Small, bottom-right, semi-transparent

## Content Differentiation — What Makes You Different

The market is crowded. Here's your edge:

1. **Real infrastructure:** You have a 5x RTX 3090 GPU server, homelab, multiple machines. Most YouTubers rent cloud GPUs or use toy setups. You show REAL hardware doing REAL work.

2. **Security + AI crossover:** Very few creators credibly bridge both. Your MCPScan project, CrowdSec setup, and security background give you authority others don't have.

3. **UK perspective:** The AI/tech YouTube space is dominated by US creators. A UK voice stands out — different references, different humour, different audience.

4. **Builder, not reviewer:** You don't unbox products. You build things that solve problems. Every video should end with something that WORKS.

5. **The homelab angle:** Jeff Geerling + NetworkChuck + AI. Self-hosted AI, local LLMs, homelab security — this niche is exploding and underserved.
