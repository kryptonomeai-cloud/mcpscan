# Growth Plan — TAYoutube

## The Algorithm (What Actually Matters)

YouTube recommends videos based on these signals (in order of importance):

1. **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Thumbnail + title → do people click?
2. **Average View Duration (AVD):** Do they stay? Target: 50%+ of video length
3. **Session time:** Do viewers watch MORE YouTube after your video?
4. **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, subscribers gained

**Translation:** Make people click (thumbnail), make them stay (pacing), make them come back (consistency).

## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Targeting
Every video should target 1-2 searchable keywords:
- **High volume, low competition:** "self-host AI locally", "homelab 2026", "LoRA fine-tuning guide"
- **Trending + evergreen combo:** "local LLM setup" (trending NOW, people will still search in 6 months)
- **Problem-solving:** "how to run LLM on RTX 3090", "Docker beginner tutorial"

### Tools for Keyword Research
- **vidIQ** (free tier): See search volume + competition for keywords
- **TubeBuddy** (free tier): Tag suggestions, SEO score
- **Google Trends:** Verify topic is growing, not dying
- **YouTube search suggest:** Type your topic, note autocomplete suggestions

### Title Formulas That Work
```
"I [built/did X] (and here's what happened)"
"How to [achieve X] in [time] (step by step)"
"[Number] [things] Every [person] Needs"
"Why [unexpected claim] (proof)"
"[Tool/Tech] Changed Everything"
```

## Community Building

### Comments (Non-Negotiable)
- Reply to EVERY comment for the first 100 videos
- Pin the best comment (makes commenter feel special → loyalty)
- Ask questions at the end of videos ("What should I build next?")
- Heart genuine comments

### Cross-Platform
- **Twitter/X:** Share behind-the-scenes, quick tips, engage with tech community
- **Reddit:** Post in r/homelab, r/selfhosted, r/LocalLLaMA, r/cybersecurity (add value, don't spam)
- **GitHub:** Link projects from videos, accept community contributions
- **Discord:** Start a community server once you hit 1K subs

### Collaborations
- Start commenting on similar creators' videos NOW
- Reach out after 5-10 published videos with specific collab ideas
- Target creators at similar size (not 1M+ channels — they won't respond)
- Local UK tech creators are underrepresented — find them

## Monetisation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Free (Month 0-3)
- No revenue focus. Build content library and audience.
- Affiliate links in descriptions (Amazon, tool referrals) — passive

### Phase 2: YouTube Partner (Month 3-6)
- Requires: 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours (or 1,000 subs + 10M Shorts views)
- Ad revenue: £1-5 per 1,000 views in tech niche (tech CPM is high)

### Phase 3: Sponsors (Month 6+)
- Tech sponsors pay well: £500-2,000+ per video at 10K-50K subs
- Target: VPN companies, cloud providers, dev tools, hardware brands
- Only sponsor products you'd actually use (trust > money)

### Phase 4: Products (Year 1+)
- Courses (homelab setup, AI deployment, security hardening)
- Consulting
- Paid community/Discord tier

## Upload Timing

Best times for tech content (UK audience + US overlap):
- **Saturday 2:00 PM GMT** — weekend viewers, US morning
- **Tuesday 5:00 PM GMT** — midweek, after work/school
- **Thursday 5:00 PM GMT** — pre-weekend
