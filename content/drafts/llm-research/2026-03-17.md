## New Releases

### Mistral Small 4 (119B) — `mistralai/Mistral-Small-4-119B-2603`
- **Provider:** Mistral AI
- **Released:** ~March 16-17, 2026 (uploaded to HF ~5 hours ago)
- **Architecture:** MoE — 128 experts, 4 active per token
- **Parameters:** 119B total, **6.5B active** per token
- **Context:** 256K tokens
- **Modalities:** Text + image input → text output
- **Key capabilities:**
  - **Unified model**: Combines Instruct, Reasoning (ex-Magistral), and Devstral (coding) into one model
  - Configurable `reasoning_effort` per request (none → high)
  - Native function calling + JSON output
  - Multimodal vision understanding
  - Speculative decoding via trained eagle head
  - NVFP4 quantized checkpoint available
- **Performance:** 40% reduction in end-to-end latency vs Mistral Small 3; 3× throughput. Claims to match/surpass "GPT-OSS 120B" on benchmarks with shorter output.
- **License:** Apache 2.0 ✅
- **Significance:** This is Mistral's answer to the MoE wave. One model that can do fast chat, deep reasoning, AND coding. The 6.5B active params mean it's very efficient despite the 119B total.
- **Links:** [HF](https://huggingface.co/mistralai/Mistral-Small-4-119B-2603) | [Eagle head](https://huggingface.co/mistralai/Mistral-Small-4-119B-2603-eagle) | [NVFP4](https://huggingface.co/mistralai/Mistral-Small-4-119B-2603-NVFP4)

### MiroThinker 1.7 — `miromind-ai/MiroThinker-1.7`
- **Provider:** MiroMind AI
- **Released:** ~March 16, 2026 (~23 hours ago)
- **Sizes:** 30B (mini) and 235B
- **Context:** 256K
- **Max tool calls:** 300 per task
- **Key results:**
  - BrowseComp: 74.0%
  - BrowseComp-ZH: 75.3% (SOTA among open-source)
  - GAIA-Val-165: 82.7%
  - HLE-Text: 42.9%
- **Focus:** Deep research agent — long-chain reasoning with step-verifiable outputs
- **Significance:** Purpose-built for agentic research tasks. Claims to outperform OpenAI DeepResearch. The 30B mini variant could be interesting for local deployment.
- **Links:** [HF 235B](https://huggingface.co/miromind-ai/MiroThinker-1.7) | [HF 30B](https://huggingface.co/miromind-ai/MiroThinker-1.7-mini) | [Demo](https://dr.miromind.ai/)
